To continue is power

Our dojo is an example of the incredible legacy left behind by the legendary Iwami Takeo Sensei. His life’s motto, “to continue is power,” is the foundation upon which we build our practice each and every day. Though we may no longer have his physical presence with us, his spirit and legacy live on in our hearts and in the very walls of this dojo. We are honored to carry on the work of such a remarkable role model who practiced until his final days. As we step onto the mat each day, we do so with a deep sense of respect and gratitude for the inspiration that is Iwami Takeo Sensei. His example reminds us that anything is possible when we approach life with determination, discipline, and a willingness to continue on even in the face of difficulty.

training programs

Juniors A

white belts / beginners

Join our Judo class for white belts and beginners to learn fundamental techniques from experienced instructors. Our program is suitable for 6 years and above, offering challenges to help build strength, agility, and coordination. Whether you want to compete or learn self-defense and improve your fitness, you’ll have a fantastic time learning Judo with us.

juniors b

intermediate / advanced color belts

Our Judo class provides comprehensive training for juniors and intermediate players. The experienced instructors offer personalized attention and guidance, focusing on basic techniques and gradually advancing to more advanced tactics. The curriculum includes throwing, grappling, submissions, ground work, fitness, and conditioning, building confidence, discipline, and respect. Whether you aim to improve Judo skills or fitness, we welcome you to join us and achieve your full potential.

adults

All Ages

Judo is a fun sport that can help you improve your body, mind and soul, no matter what experience you have. Our class has great teachers who know everything about Judo and will teach you well. You’ll learn safe ways to throw and lock your opponent, and these moves will help with your fitness and self-defense skills. Plus, you’ll become part of a friendly community with people who love Judo just like you. Don’t miss out on the chance to enjoy Judo and everything it can give you. Join us today, we’d love to have you with us.

Ladies

All Ages

This is a class for women to learn judo. It is taught by women for women. This class is good for you if you are just starting or if you already know some judo. It will be a challenge, but you will be with other women who want to learn judo too. You can be a competitor or you can learn the Kata side of Judo. This class will make you feel powerful, so don’t miss out!
