Typhoon Signal and Rainstorm warning signal arrangement

  • If Typhoon Warning Signal No. 1 or No. 3/Amber or Red Rainstorm Signal is up, dojo will continue to operate as normal, however, please kindly assess if it is safe for you or your children to attend classes due to the severe weather.
  • For T8 (or above) or Black Rainstorm signal, the dojo will be closed
  • When the bad weather signal is cancelled, dojo will resume operation within 2 hours, and classes will resume 2 hours after the signal change
  • If the signal is lowered after 6pm, dojo will remain closed for the rest of the day
  • Your safety is of utmost importance no matter what the signal is. If in doubts, stay at a safe location.
